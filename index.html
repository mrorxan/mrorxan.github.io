Filter

Find exactly what you want with improved filters

To find the right person, we first need to know what we expect from them. Especially in the activity section, make sure that you choose activities that you not only like but also want to do.

Laters

If it's not a good time, talk later

Sometimes we find someone we are looking for, but it may not be the right time. You can add them to the Laters section and interact with them again whenever you want.

Message

You can only send one message until the other party respond

We have developed a system where you can send a direct message to the person you find instead of liking them. However, when you write for the first time, you only have the right to send one message.

For University Students Only

Liberge is an application that only university students can access. We use the e-mail addresses determined by universities for students as keys to prevent outsiders from accessing them. If you have a key too, you know where the lock is!

More Features

Expand your social network

Socializing is an important part of university life. The best way to socialize is to do activities together. You can expand your circle of friends by finding activity partners with Liberge. You can meet people with whom you share common values, both from your own university and from different universities.

Liberge is not a date application

The purpose of this application is to help you find friends with whom you can do activities and have a good time. This application will not be the right choice for those looking for a Date application, they can turn to other applications that offer this service. Using the application for its intended purpose will provide you with a better experience.

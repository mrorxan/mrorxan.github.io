Features

For University Students Only

Liberge is an application that only university students can access. We use the e-mail addresses determined by universities for students as keys to prevent outsiders from accessing them. If you have a key too, you know where the lock is!

Get the App

Expand your social network

Socializing is an important part of university life. The best way to socialize is to do activities together. You can expand your circle of friends by finding activity partners with Liberge. You can meet people with whom you share common values, both from your own university and from different universities.

Liberge is not a date application

The purpose of this application is to help you find friends with whom you can do activities and have a good time. This application will not be the right choice for those looking for a Date application, they can turn to other applications that offer this service. Using the application for its intended purpose will provide you with a better experience.

Filters

You can use filters to find exactly the person you are looking for. Especially with the Activities filtering, you will find suitable people according to the activities you want to do.

Filters

You can not only be limited to your own university, but also communicate with students from different universities and maybe do online activities. It is also possible to filter according to the degree, department and year you want.

Laters

The people you want to talk to later appear on the Laters page. You can also get organized by adding the people you really want to talk to here.

Message

You can only send one message until the other party respond. Start a conversation and open doors to opportunities.

Chats

The people you messaged and the messages written to you are displayed in the Chats section. You can expand your conversation by responding to messages.

Chats

There are reply, block and report features in chats. You can give feedback by reporting people you think are behaving inappropriately.

Profile

Your profile, settings, and support sections are accessed from the Profile page. To create a more reliable impression, you can improve your profile by trying to complete your progress bar by hundred percent.

Profile

You can easily change your photos, activities, biography and academic information from your profile.

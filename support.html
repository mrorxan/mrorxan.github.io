Support

Is Liberge a paid application?

Liberge is a completely free application and does not require any payment from you.

Can anyone participate in the application?

No. Only university students can access the application. When you sign up to check this, we only accept university emails.

Can fake profiles be created?

Liberge is a reliable application and there is no need for people to create fake profiles. People who create fake profiles are detected and blocked, and thanks to our face detection feature, it is very difficult to create such situations. If you see a suspicious profile, do not hesitate to report it to our help service.

I don't have a university email, how do I get one?

If you do not have a university e-mail address and you are a student, you can request your unique student e-mail address by contacting the relevant department of your university. If you do not know where to go, you can first contact the Student Affairs Office to find out how to obtain your e-mail.

I cannot log in with my university email. What is the reason?

If you are a university student and cannot log in with your student e-mail, first check whether your university is among the options during registration. If the name of your university does not appear, it means that Liberge has not reached your university yet. We are a rapidly developing application and we will provide access to your university in a short time, we ask for your patience. If your university is displayed but you still cannot log in, there may be a problem with the extension of your e-mail, you can give us feedback for this.

All the profiles I came across are exhausted, what should I do?

Make sure you take better advantage of filtering. When you look for someone with very specific features, you will eventually come across fewer profiles. It would be beneficial to appeal to a wider range of people when filtering. This way, you can meet more people and start a conversation by choosing the one that suits you.
